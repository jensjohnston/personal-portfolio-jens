Airinum

Urban health necessories since 2015

Airinum is a Swedish health brand founded in 2015. Ever since, Airinum has provided people with innovative everyday accessories that reduce the health effects of climate change.

Responsibilites

Design, development, analysis, testing and project management

What I do

My main responsibilities is to conduct research, UX, designing interfaces and coding. I like Airbnb Design's quote, three elements define a product, the business, the code and the pixels. So I do a bit more than just the UX and design but also focusing more on business needs.

I'm responsible for the development team working with 2 developers creating developer-ready assets and components in Figma while also doing the dev myself (HTML, CSS, Liquid, React and JS).

I've built the entire Airinum UI Ecommerce design system ranging from bigger components, to the smallest building stones.
